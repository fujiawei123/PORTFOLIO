ABOUT MOONTON

Shanghai MOONTON Technology Co. Ltd. (Chinese: 上海沐瞳科技有限公司), commonly known as MOONTON Games, is a Chinese multinational video game developer and publisher owned by the Nuverse subsidiary of ByteDance and based in Shanghai, China. It is best known for the mobile multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game Mobile Legends: Bang Bang released in July 2016.

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, commonly referred to as ML or MLBB, is a mobile multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton, the game grew in popularity worldwide, most prominently in Southeast Asia.



MKT STRATEGY

Bang Bang's overseas marketing strategy focuses on localization and flexibility, adapting to infrastructure limitations by building internet servers and working with local partners. Their communication strategy involves partnering with local KOLs to create content, advertising on social media, and building small communities based on localized content to form a loyal following. MOONTON has worked with over 1,000 KOLs worldwide to create diverse content for different channels, and they have recognized the overlap between e-sports and TikTok user groups, actively recruiting TikTok creators and engaging with e-sports brands and clubs in the MLBB e-competition.
